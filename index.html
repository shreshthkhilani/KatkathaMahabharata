Katkatha decided to develop their own adaptation of the Mahabharata in October, 2015. Anurupa brought the idea to the group in a meeting and they all agreed that after About Ram, this was a challenge they were willing to take on.

The performers for the show are: Anurupa Roy, Avinash Kumar, Gunduraju, Mohammad Shameem, and Vivek Kumar. Anurupa, Asha, and Shammem built the puppets; Avinash is the choreographer; Atul is the graphics designer.
